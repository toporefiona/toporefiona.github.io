This project involved leading the technical design of a sustainable 
healthcare facility using BIM as part of my Integrated Building Design 
module at Cardiff University. I coordinated MEP, structural and 
architectural systems across all design stages, managing the 
interdisciplinary dependencies that arise when teams work in parallel. 
The project gave me the most project-like experience of my degree, 
developing my ability to manage multidisciplinary coordination and 
deliver to regulatory standards within a BIM environment.
