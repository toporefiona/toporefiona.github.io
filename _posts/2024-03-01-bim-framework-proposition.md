This project developed a BIM coordination strategy focused on seismic 
resilience and lifecycle structural verification across a multidisciplinary 
infrastructure project. The work explored the overlap between digital 
delivery tools and structural safety, covering multidisciplinary 
coordination, structural flaw detection and lifecycle verification. 
It strengthened my understanding of how information management 
underpins safe and efficient project delivery across complex 
engineering programmes.
